Privacy Policy for BlockZap

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

Thank you for playing BlockZap, developed by Synctegron. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Collected Automatically

When you play BlockZap, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or, on iOS, the Advertising Identifier / IDFA), and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Diagnostic data, crash reports, and performance analytics to help us improve the game.

B. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The third-party service providers used by the app include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob uses the device's Advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on user interests.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase to track crashes, bugs, and game performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our game.
  • To serve advertisements (including personalized ads) to keep the game free to play.
  • To monitor the usage of our game and detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

3. Data Safety & Advertising ID

Android: Our app declares the com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID permission in its manifest. This allows our advertising partners (AdMob) to provide relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising by going to your Android Device Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

iOS: Our app may request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission to access your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA), which AdMob uses to serve more relevant ads. You can allow or deny this the first time you open the app, and you can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you decline or disable tracking, ads will still be shown, but they may be less personalized.

4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
